- To select the source of the sound to be played, click on the speaker icon.
Only one sound can be activated at the same time.
- To save the sound, select the observer (click on the speaker icon of the selected observer).
Run animation by clicking START. Then click the floppy disk button.
- To increase the active simulation area, extend the window.
- The range of frequencies you hear depends on your playback equipment.
If you can not hear the sound, try changing the frequency of the source.
